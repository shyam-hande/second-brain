# Productivity Tips

## Second Brain Concept
A second brain is an external system to store and organize knowledge.
Popularized by Tiago Forte in Building a Second Brain.
Key principles: capture, organize, distill, express (CODE).

## Note Taking
Always write notes in your own words.
Link related concepts together.
Review notes regularly to reinforce learning.

## Deep Work
Block distractions for focused work sessions.
Use time blocking to protect important work time.
Start with the hardest task first thing in the morning.

## Learning Effectively
Active recall is better than passive reading.
Spaced repetition helps long term memory.
Teaching others solidifies your own understanding.
