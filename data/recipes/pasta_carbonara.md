# Pasta Carbonara

## Ingredients
- 400g spaghetti
- 200g guanciale (or pancetta)
- 4 egg yolks
- 100g Pecorino Romano
- Black pepper
- Salt

## Steps
1. Cook pasta until al dente
2. Fry guanciale until crispy
3. Mix egg yolks with cheese
4. Combine pasta with guanciale OFF heat
5. Add egg mixture, toss quickly
6. Serve immediately with black pepper

## Notes
Never add cream! The creaminess comes from the eggs and pasta water.
